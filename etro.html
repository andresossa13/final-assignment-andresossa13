ETRO

Product Sizing for Luxury eCommerce

Through extensive research and understanding of the challenges in digital retail, my goal was to devise a successful strategy that would simplify the process of selecting sizes and colors, in order to help costumers understand how garments fit correctly.

This involved analyzing and rethinking the way product sizes are displayed and explained, ultimately making it easier for customers to make their choices. The project presented a challenge in exploring innovative ways to visualize products and enhance the overall digital experience.

After conducting thorough research on the brand, its customers, purchasing processes, and main competitors, I focused on understanding its values and digital presence. Furthermore, I had the opportunity to visit one of their primary boutiques situated in the fashion center of Milan. During my visit, I interviewed two saleswomen—one responsible for physical sales and the other for online sales.

This experience provided me with a deeper understanding of how a brand of this caliber operates and what value I could bring to the project. The final proposal to the client consisted of three parts. The first part aimed to draw viewers’ attention to the most important elements, messages, and calls to action. Introducing the option to change the size and color of the product directly from the landing page, along with two new buttons that lead to the new proposals I developed specifically for the brand.

Fit Visualization – An interactive guide that provides customers with information about different Etro garments. By completing a series of questions, the software presents various garments tailored to the customer’s body type as represented in the guide.

Color and Outfit Variations – This section assists customers in matching the selected garment with different available color variations. It also allows them to combine it with other etro garments to create unique outfits.
