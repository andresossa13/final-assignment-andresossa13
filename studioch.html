A harmonious living environment

Welcome to the Cuesta Hurtado family project, a young couple who, with the addition of a new member to their family, are continuing to establish their cozy home.

This family firmly believes that each space within their home serves a primary function, and although occasionally utilized for multiple purposes simultaneously, it is crucial to prioritize and amplify its intended purpose to foster a harmonious living environment.

Considering their belief, our utmost priority was to design a versatile workspace that offers the option of independent or combined desks. Furthermore, we incorporated dedicated storage cabinets and compartments for electrical cables and extensions. Lastly, we aimed to create functional and open surfaces to showcase books and decorations.
