BOOK COVER

Enhancing the visual appeal

The purpose of this exercise was to enhance the understanding and visual appeal of a book cover by utilizing serif and sans serif fonts as the main design elements. The book I chose to work on was "When" by Daniel H. Pink.

Daniel H. Pink, a renowned author and the #1 bestseller of “Drive” and “To Sell Is Human,” reveals the scientific secrets behind optimal timing to help individuals thrive in their professional, academic, and personal lives. While we acknowledge the importance of timing, our knowledge about it remains limited.

The decisions we make throughout our lives revolve around the question of “when”: when to launch a business, when to schedule a class, when to commit to a relationship. However, these decisions often rely on intuition and guesswork, with timing being regarded as an art form. In his book, “When: The Scientific Secrets of Perfect Timing,” Pink demonstrates that timing is, in fact, a science.
