SANE

The dating app with a twist

The brief for this project had a unique approach. I was given the word "hate" and tasked with creating a project relevant to my field of study. leveraging my knowledge in digital design, I decided to develop a mobile dating app entirely from scratch, encompassing its visual identity, content, and functional prototype.

Introducing Sane, the app to meet people with a twist. Typically, individuals join dating apps with the intention of finding someone based on pictures or positive attributes. However, Sane presents an alternative approach.

The name “Sane” (pronounced “saw-nay”) derives from the Hebrew word for hate. In ancient Hebrew culture, hate was understood as being wounded or hurt by something or someone due to the presence of love being involved. Expressing emotions meant opening oneself up to potential pain.

The app’s concept revolves around making choices that minimize the risk of being hurt. When people are driven by strong emotions to share their negative opinions, it results in the formation of deeper connections. Research has revealed that individuals forge stronger bonds when they are able to discuss their dislikes rather than when both parties share positive feelings towards something.
