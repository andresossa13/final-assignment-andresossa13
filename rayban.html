RAY-BAN STORIES

Enhancing the user experience

The project presented the challenge of enhancing the user experience on the mobile version of the Ray-Ban stories website.

We began by conducting various user testing sessions to identify common patterns in users’ interactions with the site. The sessions yielded valuable insights that provided us with a better understanding of how the target users navigate the website and perceive the product information, taking into account user types and their specific needs. Based on the research findings, we identified the following areas of the website as opportunities for design intervention:

1. Landing Page: Our goal was to enhance the loading screen, making it both useful and interactive. Additionally, we aimed to personalize the user experience by allowing users to select different styles right from the beginning.

2. Discovery Page: We sought to simplify the entire page, reducing clutter and improving navigation. We aimed to establish consistency between the user interface and the interaction experience. To achieve this, we highlighted the submenu for increased visibility and created a new section to showcase real stories that feature the product. Furthermore, we emphasized the product’s key features.

3. Product Detail Page: Our focus was on improving the product detail page by finding a more efficient way to display filters for frame colors and lens combinations. Additionally, we reorganized the arrangement of the “all product” display.
