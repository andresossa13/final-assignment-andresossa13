STUDIO T110

Distribution is the beginning

Welcome to Juan Carlos & Natalia's Home Office. This Couple sought to create a unique ambiance in their workspace, aiming for increased comfort during the pandemic.

For this project, we carefully considered several factors. Our primary focus was on harnessing natural light and optimizing the visual perspective of the space. We also prioritized establishing smooth circulation between rooms and conducted a thorough analysis of storage requirements. Moreover, we took into account the couple’s lifestyle and personal preferences as key elements in the design.

The outcome of the project surpassed the couple’s expectations. We incorporated a vertical shelf to elegantly showcase books, decorations, vines, and various plants. Additionally, we created a dedicated area for displaying wine. To enhance the overall design, we strategically implemented room divisions and gradually reduced the visual field towards the bedroom. Furthermore, we crafted two separate work areas with a seamless and continuous surface. These areas feature shared drawers that effectively divide the two spaces.
