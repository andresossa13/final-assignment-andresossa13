PINN AIR

Airline for Tristan Da Cunha

The concept brand is a small carrierr airline that connects the island of tristan da cunha with the 2 main airports of south africa and south america – cape town and buenos aires as well as the in between laying islands.

Known by Tristan Islanders as ‘PINNAMINS’, Northern Rockhopper Penguins breed in rookeries throughout the Tristan archipelago. Inspired by this unique island mascot, the airline was given a name – PINN AIR (abbreviated to make a bold statement and easier to remember).

PINN AIR is aimed at connecting the local community and aligning specific tourism with the islands beauty and wonder. A new smaller carrier that offers safe, reliable and considered approach to travel with a contemporary and progressive point of view. Tristan Da Cunha’s remote location and nature focused values position the brand in a space where it can offer an exclusive connection to curate experiences for specific purposes. The isolation gives an edge and a voice to stand out and carve out a unique space in the travel industry. The brand’s vision is to reveal the hidden wonders of our world, with the mission to connect the remote with the curious through a nature conscious lense, looking to discover something unique.

My Role

As a member of the team, my primary responsibility was to translate the brand concept into visually compelling digital experiences that effectively conveyed the brand’s essence and values. During the initial stages of the project, I collaborated closely with the branding team to understand the brand’s objectives, target audience, and desired aesthetic.

Drawing inspiration from the brand’s identity and vision, I contributed to the development of the design concept, crafting a cohesive and engaging visual language that resonated with the brand’s identity. Once the design concept was finalized, I took the lead in translating it into tangible digital assets.
